During the year under report 15,063 births were registered in Ajmer- Merwara in 1919 against 13,214 in the previous year, an increase of 1,849. 4. Of the total births registered 8,021 were males and 7,042 females. The birth rate was 30.04 per mille of population as compared with 26.35 per mille in 1918. The percentage of male to female births was 113.90 against 119.17 in the previous year. Deaths in Ajmer-Merwara. 5. The total number of deaths registered in this division was 14,375 in 1919 against 57,553 in 1918 a decrease of 43,178 due to absence of any large epide- mic. The death rate per mille of population was 28.67 as compared with 114.78 in the previous year. 6. The mortality among children under five years of age was 43 per cent. of the total deaths against 19 per cent, in the previous year. 7. 11,054 deaths from &quot;Fevers&quot; were reported against 47,803 in 1918, an decrease of 36,749 due chiefly to the absence of Influenza.
