During the year from 1st April 1914 to 31st March 1915, 570 cases of plague and 458 deaths were reported in 44 towns and villages infected in the Native States in Rajputana against 2,286 seizures and 1,885 deaths in 103 towns and villages in 1913-14. The District of Ajmer- Merwara remained free from the disease throughout the year. The percentage of total plague deaths to population was 1.38 as compared with 1.37 in the previous year (vide table attached). Native States. 60. The disease lingered from the preceding year until May, 1914 re- appeared in November and is still in existence.
